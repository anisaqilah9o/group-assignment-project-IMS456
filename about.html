ABOUT US

  • She.World offers you an online purchasing service which allows you to purchase a selection of the items available at our store from your computer or mobile devices. Our company was launched in 2020 in IOI Mall, Puchong. We have since expanded to more than 5 stores across Malaysia, spanning from Peninsular Malaysia to East Malaysia. Throughout the years, our company has cemented its reputation as a provider of quality garments and accessories at the most competitive price. At She.World website, we aim to bring about better values and an elevated online shopping experience, all from the convenience of your home. You will be able to find products such as blouse, palazo, dress and many more. Catering for women including plus sizes, we are confident that you will find something to your liking here at She.World website.
